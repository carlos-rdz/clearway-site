Data Processing Addendum

Last updated: June 2026.

Starter template. This document is a working draft and not yet reviewed by counsel — have a qualified attorney review and adapt it before relying on it.

1. Roles

For personal data processed under the Service, Customer is the controller (or processor on behalf of its own customers) and Clearway is the processor (or sub-processor). This DPA forms part of the Terms of Service.

2. Scope & nature of processing

Clearway processes Customer data solely to provide the Service — rendering clearances, maintaining the catalog, and producing audit records — and on Customer's documented instructions.

3. Customer instructions

Clearway will process personal data only as instructed by Customer and as needed to provide the Service, unless required by law.

4. Confidentiality

Personnel authorized to process personal data are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Security

Clearway maintains technical and organizational measures appropriate to the risk, including encryption in transit and at rest, tenant isolation, least-privilege access, and append-only audit logging. See Security.

6. Subprocessors

Customer authorizes Clearway to engage subprocessors (see Security for categories). Clearway remains responsible for subprocessors and will give notice of changes and a chance to object.

7. Data subject requests

Clearway will assist Customer in responding to data-subject requests (access, deletion, correction, portability) to the extent Customer cannot do so itself through the Service.

8. Personal data breach

Clearway will notify Customer without undue delay after becoming aware of a personal data breach affecting Customer data, with information reasonably available.

9. Return or deletion

On termination, Clearway will delete or return Customer data per the Terms, subject to legal retention requirements.

10. International transfers

Where applicable, the parties will rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other valid transfer mechanisms.

11. Audits

Clearway will make available information necessary to demonstrate compliance and allow for audits consistent with applicable law, subject to confidentiality and reasonable scheduling.

12. Precedence

In case of conflict regarding personal data, this DPA prevails over the Terms.

13. Contact

Data protection: privacy@clearwayagents.ai.
