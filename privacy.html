Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026.

Starter template. This document is a working draft and not yet reviewed by counsel — have a qualified attorney review and adapt it before relying on it.

1. Scope

This policy explains how Clearway Agents ("Clearway", "we") handles information when you visit our website or use the Service. For customer data processed on behalf of a business customer, our Data Processing Addendum governs.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information — name, work email, organization.
  • Product & program configuration — the product profiles and settings you submit to be cleared.
  • Usage & log data — API requests, timestamps, and diagnostics.
  • Website data — basic analytics and essential cookies.

3. How we use information

To provide and secure the Service, render clearances, support you, and improve the product. We do not use customer data to train models, and model inference runs under zero-retention terms.

4. How we share

We share with subprocessors that help us run the Service (see Security), when required by law, or in connection with a business transfer. We do not sell personal information.

5. Security

We encrypt data in transit and at rest, isolate tenants, and log access. See Security.

6. Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the Service and meet legal obligations, then delete or de-identify it. Customers can configure retention for cleared records.

7. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including under the GDPR and CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Contact us to exercise them.

8. International transfers

Where we transfer personal data across borders, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies and limited, privacy-respecting analytics. You can control non-essential cookies in your browser.

10. Children

The Service is for businesses and not directed to children; we do not knowingly collect children's data.

11. Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be communicated.

12. Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@clearwayagents.ai.
