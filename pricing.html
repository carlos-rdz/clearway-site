FAQ

Common questions.

How is it priced?

By clearances run and programs covered — the volume of review you no longer do by hand — not by seats. We'll size it against your launch cadence on a call.

Is there a sandbox?

Yes. Design partners and Growth customers get a sandbox key to run clearances against sample product profiles before going live. See the docs.

Are you a regulated entity?

No. Clearway provides software tooling, not legal advice, and does not hold funds or move money. The bank and fintech remain the obligated parties; our determinations support their program.

What about our data?

Isolated per tenant, encrypted, and never used to train models. See Security and our DPA.
